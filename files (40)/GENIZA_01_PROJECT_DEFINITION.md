# GENIZA — Project Definition
**Trust-first collector exchange. France first. Mid-market only.**

---

## What Is GENIZA

GENIZA is a hybrid marketplace for serious collectors trading art, antiques, bandes dessinées, and cards priced between €200 and €8,000.

The mechanic no one else has: **trade-first, cash second.**

You list what you own. You browse what others own. You propose a trade — my Moebius print for your Bilal original. If the values don't match, you add cash to close the gap. At any point, you can also list items for straight cash sale.

Your collection IS your public profile. Trust is built through what you own, not what you claim.

---

## The Gap GENIZA Fills

| Platform | Problem |
|---|---|
| Le Bon Coin | No trust, no curation, no collector identity |
| eBay | Generic, impersonal, race to bottom on price |
| Drouot / Christie's | Entry starts at €8,000+. Most collectors are locked out |
| Catawiki | Auction-only, slow, generic categories |

**The orphaned market:** Everything priced €200–€8,000. That is where 90% of real collectors actually live.

---

## Who GENIZA Is For

**Primary user:** A French collector aged 28–55. Owns 10–200 objects. Knows what things are worth. Wants to upgrade their collection without always paying cash. Distrusts anonymous platforms. Values provenance and authenticity.

**Categories at launch:**
- Art prints and original works (lithographies, sérigraphies, illustrations)
- Antiques and objets d'art (brocante, numismatique, vintage luxury)
- Bandes dessinées (Tintin, Moebius, Bilal, Druillet — original plates and signed editions)
- Trading cards (Pokémon FR, vintage sports)

> **Echange ou vends ta collection. Sans intermédiaire inutile.**
> (Trade or sell your collection. No unnecessary middleman.)

In English: **The only marketplace built for collectors who trade, not just buy.**

---

## The Trust Layer (What Makes This Hard to Copy)

1. **Verified listings.** Every item requires minimum 4 photos + condition grade (Excellent / Bon / Acceptable). Optional: upload certificate, invoice, or grading slip.
2. **Collector score.** Built from completed trades + community confirmations. Not reviews — verifications.
3. **Escrow on every transaction.** Payment held until buyer confirms receipt. No cash released before confirmation.
4. **Trade proposal system.** Formal structured offer: Item A + optional cash for Item B. Both parties confirm. Escrow activates on both sides.
5. **Provenance field.** Where did you get it. When. From whom. Optional but surfaced prominently.

---

## Revenue Model

**Transaction fee: 6% on completed sales. 4% on completed trades (both sides).**

No listing fees at launch. Friction kills early supply.

Future: Authentication badge (partner with PSA or local expert network). Premium seller profile. Featured listing slots.

---

## UX Principles (Builder Must Follow)

### Bezos: Work backwards from the customer
Every screen must answer one question: what does the collector want to do right now? The answer is always: browse, list, or propose. Three actions. No more.

### Jobs: Clarity over cleverness
The value proposition must be visible in 3 seconds without scrolling. A first-time visitor must understand what GENIZA does before they click anything.

### Musk: Remove every step that is not necessary
Listing flow: 5 steps maximum (photos, details, category, price or trade preference, publish). If a step can be eliminated, eliminate it.

### Amodei: Systems that scale without chaos
The database schema must handle future categories without migration hell. The trade proposal system must be atomic — both sides confirm or neither side confirms.

---

## Language and Tone

- French first. Every label, error, and empty state in French.
- English toggle available but not default at launch.
- No marketing speak. No "revolutionizing the collector experience."
- Direct, like a knowledgeable dealer. "Condition: Bon. Quelques traces d'usage sur la couverture."
- Prices always in EUR.

---

## What GENIZA Is Not

- Not an auction house
- Not a social network (the social graph is a consequence of collection overlap, not a feature)
- Not a grading service (we surface grades, we do not issue them)
- Not a storage solution
- Not for mass-produced items or counterfeits (zero tolerance)

## 6-Month Roadmap

**Month 1–2 (MVP):** Listings, search, cash sale, user profiles with collection view.
**Month 3:** Trade proposal system + escrow integration.
**Month 4:** Condition grading UI + provenance field + AI-assisted description.
**Month 5:** Collector score system + verified badge.
**Month 6:** BD/cards category expansion + mobile-first polish.
